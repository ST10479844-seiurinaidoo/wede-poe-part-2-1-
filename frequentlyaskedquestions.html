Frequently Asked Questions:

Where every ray powers a brighter tomorrow...

General Questions

  • Q: How do solar panels actually work?

    A: Solar panels capture sunlight and convert it into electricity through photovoltaic cells. The energy is then used in your home or stored in batteries for later use.

  • Q: Can solar panels power my entire house?

    A: Yes, depending on the size of the system installed. A properly designed solar system can cover 100% of your household energy needs.

  • Q: Will solar panels still work during load shedding or power cuts?

    A: Standard solar systems shut down during outages for safety reasons, but with a battery storage system, you can keep essential appliances running even during load shedding.

Maintenance & Performance

  • Q: How much maintenance do solar panels need?

    A: Very little. Panels generally just need occasional cleaning to remove dust or leaves, and we recommend an inspection every 1–2 years.

  • Q: What happens if it hails or the panels get damaged?

    A: Solar panels are designed to withstand harsh weather. In the rare case of damage, our team offers repair and replacement services, and most systems are covered by warranty.

  • Q: How long is the warranty on solar panels?

    A: Most panels come with a 20 to 25 year performance warranty, ensuring reliable energy production for decades.

Installation

  • Q: How long does installation take?

    A: Most residential solar systems can be installed in 1 to 3 days depending on the size and complexity of the project.

  • Q: Do I need a specific type of roof for solar panels?

    A: Solar panels can be installed on most roof types including tile, metal, and flat roofs. During consultation, we’ll check your roof’s suitability.

  • Q: Will installing solar panels damage my roof?

    A: No. Panels are securely mounted with professional equipment, and proper installation ensures your roof remains protected.

Costs & Savings

  • Q: How much do solar panels cost?

    A: Costs depend on system size, roof type, and whether batteries are included. We provide customized quotes to match your budget and energy needs.

  • Q: Will solar panels really save me money?

    A: Yes! Many households see a 30 to 70% reduction in energy bills. With rising electricity costs, the long-term savings are significant.

  • Q: Is financing or payment plans available?

    A: Absolutely. We offer flexible financing options to make solar energy more affordable and accessible for everyone.

Environmental Benefits

  • Q: How much carbon can I save by switching to solar?

    A: A typical residential solar system can offset 3 to 4 tons of carbon emissions each year – the equivalent of planting over 100 trees annually.

  • Q: Does using solar energy really make a difference?

    A: Yes! Every household that switches to solar reduces demand for fossil fuels, helping fight climate change and creating a cleaner planet.
